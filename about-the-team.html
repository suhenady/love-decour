Meet the Team

Two friends + One love for design = Love DeCour

Meet Herbertta Thomas. Get in touch with me!

Hello, my name is Herbertta Thomas. I am Co-owner of Love DeCour. I am a Proud Liberian American. I come from an amazing family full of Love, Compassion, Trust, and Integrity. I am the second to the youngest of 7 children. I am married to a wonderful man and we share 2 incredible children and 1 fur baby.

Before event décor, I worked in Property Management. I am a graduate of Valdosta State University where I earned my BA in Political Science and my 2nd BA in Sociology. I then went on to earn my MBA from Keller University and a MA in Project Management.

My Love of event décor started in college when I would put together events and set stage for our modeling organization. As the years went on I began to help set up events for family and friends and the joy I got from this was unexplainable. My heart would literally smile when I saw the happiness on their faces. I realized…that was the number one reason I liked what I was doing. Seeing the excitement and amazement on the faces of everyone I helped was PRICELESS. I realized that I Truly Loved DÉCOR.

Meet Sonita Ricks. Get in touch with me!

Hello, my name is Sonita Ricks-Duyen. I am orgnally from West Africa my mother is Ghanaian and my father is Liberian. I spent most of my childhood in Ghana. I am the only girl child for my parents. I came to the United States when I was 16 years old. I obtained both my high school diploma and college degree in Minnesota, I moved to Georgia in 2008, and I have three beautiful children with my wonderful husband.

My passion for event decoration started when I was about 12 years old in Africa. I remember gathering my friends together when we went outside to play and pretended we were at an event, and I would place leaves from the tree on the table and use my mom's utensils on the table. I have expressed my passion for decorations ever since.

I am passionate about helping my clients turn their dream and vision into a reality they will never forget. I truly feel the fulfillment knowing that my clients are satisfied.
